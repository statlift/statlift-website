LinkedIn Integration (optional)

To automatically publish your weekly articles on LinkedIn, you need to grant admin rights to our LinkedIn account. Just follow these steps:

How to grant Statlift admin rights on LinkedIn:
  1. Go to your LinkedIn Company Page and click "Manage page" at the top
  2. In the left menu, click "Settings" → "Manage admins"
  3. Click "Add admin" and search for: Statlift
  4. Select the role "Super admin" and confirm
  5. Done! Once the rights are active, we'll handle the rest.

You can revoke access at any time in your LinkedIn settings.
